Sustainability

The key to the success of any enterprise is employee engagement, attraction, and retention. We believe in this too. We understand the requirements of our people to offer benefits, such as social benefits, health benefits, vacation off, flexible work hours, and work from home options. We pair these benefits with in-house training and growth opportunities for our employees.

Equal Opportunities

VIPAS believes in offering amazing opportunities to every individual. We don’t have any type of discrimination in the office related to caste, race, color, religion, marital status, age, gender, disability, or other any factor. We wish for our employees to grow and achieve career success. This is why our performance appraisal decisions are purely based only on potential, merit, performance, and competence.

Benefits

At VIPAS, you get a series of employee benefits. This includes work from home options, flexible work settings, and part-time working tradition. If our employees work hard to offer us success and revenue, we contribute to their well-being. We help our employees have a work-life balance.

We harbor a culture of inclusion and diversity at the workplace. This is achieved with supportive policies and strong workflows. We create an environment where our employees have the opportunity to grow and achieve personal access.
