Life Sciences

Rely on our SAP S/4 HANA + Industry experts for your digital journey in pharmaceuticals!

Life Sciences Industry is highly innovative and dynamic in nature. Today, life sciences stand at a critical convergence with the evolving need for personalized health services, patient-centric working models, increasingly stringent regulatory framework, improved speed to cater to market pressure, increased production flexibility to technical advancements, and rising demand for value-based patient-care. Transformation in this industry is almost inevitable. Practices and workflows which seemed productive and pertinent yesterday have lost relevance today.

In pursuit of these indispensable conversions, Pharmaceutical, Biotechnology and Medical Devices enterprises have started adoption of digital technologies to mitigate complexity, reduce operational costs - time to market, comply with global regulatory laws/policies, increase flexibility in sourcing and to stay productive.

VIPAS’s Life Sciences empowers these enterprises build a robust landscape where the market and competition dynamics are in flux. To help companies stay ahead in the competition, we implement innovative pre- configured business solution for life sciences industry, centered on delivering better customer Satisfaction at much lower cost. We drive this innovation by combining our deep life sciences experience with next- generation technological capabilities

Life Sciences

Lifepro – A ready to deploy solution for S/4 HANA

Our life sciences solution for SAP, Lifepro is proven to cover 95% of the key processes built with some of the best industry practices. It offers streamlined business processes that support your objective to standardize, centralize, and simplify your business.

Lifepro is an end-to-end business solution tailored for the life sciences industry. The package seamlessly integrates business processes across logistics and production, and ﬁnance processes important to industries engaged in the ﬁelds of life sciences, pharmaceuticals, medical devices, nutraceuticals, and biotechnology.

Our implementation method combines the best features of the V model and process-oriented system documentation compliant with GAMP5. It also uses the SAP activate methodology in collaboration with accelerators and our best industry processes.

VIPAS has an experience of over 200+ implementations in SAP ERP and 50+ life science project implementations. We are an evident choice when it comes to speedy delivery and quality solutions in SAP’s product space.
