Site Usage and User Tracking

VIPAS Techno tracks user traffic patterns. We do not collect specific data but only track information to determine which areas of our site users like and would consider visiting again. We do not track what an individual user reads or views – just how well each page performs overall. This helps us to provide quality software programming services and helps ensure customer satisfaction. VIPAS Techno does not use cookies to track user traffic patterns. Cookies are text files that are stored in the browser files on your computer.

Personal Information Sharing

VIPAS Techno does not share or distribute any information or documents about individual users with any third party, except to comply with applicable laws, valid legal processes or to protect the personal safety of our users or the public.

Security

Our site has advanced industry-standard security measures to prevent data loss, misuse, or alteration. VIPAS Techno personnel cannot access any of your personal information. VIPAS Techno will only have access to the information that was submitted using the Contact form.

Changes

VIPAS Techno reserves the right to change, alter, or update this Privacy Statement at any time basis our discretion. As a result, we encourage you to review this privacy statement periodically to track changes.
